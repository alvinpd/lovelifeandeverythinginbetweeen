Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud. It does not dishonor others, it is not self-seeking, it is not easily angered, it keeps no record of wrongs. The day I said I do to the the man who fixed me. :)

How it started:

No we did not started as friends. Rather, we started as colleagues. Looking back, it’s amazing how love can come to people at times you least expect it.

I was in a process of getting over someone when we met. He on the other hand has been in haitus for years when it comes to having a relationship with someone. He waited around a year before I finally took my chance with him. :)

Fast forward:

We were part of the LDR population. It was actually a gift because it was through it, we realized how much we wanted to be together.

To the near future:

Yes, you are uncertain. But I know, whatever you will throw at us, we can overcome it with God and with our love.

To my husband:

I love you with all my heart. Thank you for fixing my heart.
